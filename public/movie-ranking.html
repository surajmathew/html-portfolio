The Best Movies according to Movie-Dissector

My Top 3 Movies of all time

INTERSTELLAR

With humanity teetering on the brink of extinction, a group of astronauts travels through a wormhole in search of another inhabitable planet.

INCEPTION

A troubled thief who extracts secrets from people's dreams takes one last job:leading a dangerous mission to plant an idea in a target's subconscious.

MONEYBALL

Frustrated that his baseball team can't afford big-money players, Oakland A's general manager Billy Beane bets on a bold new strategy to change the game.
